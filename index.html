The Firm

Tolosa Tax is a tax preparation firm started by Karen Bolen with a passion to help small business owners and individuals manage and grow their financial well-being. With a strong accounting and tax background, the firm provides clients with expert advice, professionalism, responsiveness, and peace of mind.

Karen in an Enrolled Agent. Enrolled Agents are federally licensed tax practioners who have technical expertise in the field of taxation and who are empowered by the Department of the Treasury to represent taxpayers before all administrative levels of the IRS for audits, collections, and appeals.

About Us

Karen grew up in Southern California where she was first introduced to the tax profession by working for her dad's tax firm. She earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Economics and her Master of Science in Kinesiology at Cal Poly in San Luis Obispo. After 15 years working at Stanford University in management and accounting, Karen missed the people and quality of life of the Central Coast. She returned to San Luis Obispo several years ago and fulfilled her dream of starting a successful tax firm. When not working, Karen enjoys taking advantage of the nearby trails, mountain biking and hiking with her dogs.
